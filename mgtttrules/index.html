Monumental Gaming - Trouble in Terrorist Town Rules

Expand each rule for further information.

General Rules

  • 1. Do not harass, threaten or discriminate other players.

    1.1 - Do not grief other players or exacerbate negative situations.
    1.2 - Do not harass or discriminate based on:
      1.1.1 - Gender
      1.1.2 - Voice
      1.1.3 - Age
      1.1.4 - Race
    1.3 - Threats towards players, the server and the community will result in severe consequences.
      1.3.1 - Saying you're going to DDOS the server, joking or not, will result in a insta-permanent ban.

  • 2. Do not spam.

    2.1 - Over microphone, in text chat or by other means it is not permitted. Includes but is not limited to:
      2.1.1 - Spamming binds, more than 3 times in quick succession.
      2.1.2 - Playing music through chat.
      2.1.3 - Constantly talking without a reason.
      2.1.4 - Constantly sending messages in chat.
    2.2 - Do not use a voice changer.
    2.3 - Do not spam buttons on maps that make sounds over and over, more than 3 times in quick succession.

  • 3. Do not metagame or talk about metagaming.

    3.1 - Metagaming means giving out otherwise unknown information to other players using outside mechanics. Includes but is not limited to:
      3.1.1 - Traitor identities.
      3.1.2 - Player locations.
      3.1.3 - Who is still alive.
      3.1.4 - T-Traps or T-Weapons locations.
      3.1.5 - Unidentified body locations.
    3.2 - If you suspect a player of metagaming, use admin chat (Type "@[message]") or else you may give it away and may be gagged and/or muted.

  • 4. Do not glitch, cheat or exploit.

    4.1 - Glitching or exploiting the map, including leaving map boundaries, is not allowed.
    4.2 - Cheating with scripts or third party programs will not be tolerated.

  • 5. Traitor baiting is not KOSable, but being killed for it is not RDM.

    5.1 - Traitor baiting includes but is not limited to:
      5.1.1 - A player is shooting near players or is actually damaging players to troll.
      5.1.2 - A player is activating traps, activating T items or equipping T weapons.
      5.1.3 - A player is claiming he's a traitor.
      5.1.4 - A player is carrying an explosive barrel to a group of people (don't shoot the barrel, only the player).
    5.2 - Deliberate traitor baiting will result in consequences.

  • 6. Do not RDM (Random Death Match).

    6.1 - This means killing other players without valid reason. This also includes but is not limited to:
      6.1.1 - Firing the detective weapon, Golden Gun, without reason.
      6.1.2 - Taking it upon yourself to enforce rules. Server staff will deal with rule breakers.
    6.2 - Leaving after unpunished RDM will result in a ban.

  • 7. Have a pronounceable and typable name in English.

    7.1 - The following are examples of non-pronounceable or non-English names:
      7.1.1 - 复仇者.
      7.1.2 - _-_-_.
      7.1.3 - *^%^%$(Dhd7&^(^.
    7.2 - Do not have a username that mimics in-game callouts. Includes but is not limited to:
      7.2.1 - Someone in Disguise.
      7.2.2 - That Guy.
      7.2.3 - Nobody.
    7.3 - Failing to change your name upon the request of staff will result in a kick.

  • 8. Take reports seriously.

    8.1 - False reports will not be tolerated and will result in a slay.
    8.2 - Violent language in a report is not helpful nor tolerated and will also result in a slay.
    8.3 - Truthfully and calmly explaining a situation is more helpful to staff when handling reports.
    8.4 - A staff member's word is final, within reason.

As a Traitor

  • 9. Warn and do not damage your fellow traitors.

    9.1 - Warn fellow Traitors about any harmful actions and give sufficient time for them to vacate the targeted area. Harmful actions include but is not limited to:
      9.1.1 - Throwing a pigeon.
      9.1.2 - Throwing a holy hand grenade.
    9.2 - You do not need to warn for traitor items that are visible on the map (e.g. C4, melon mines, SLAM).

  • 10. Do not delay.

    10.1 - In overtime, camping or deliberately stalling will result in a slay.

As an Innocent/Detective

  • 11. Do not shoot or damage somebody without solid evidence.

    11.1 - Solid evidence includes but is not limited to:
      11.1.1 - A KOS (Kill on Sight) was called.
      11.1.2 - DNA from a body.
      11.1.3 - Witnessing an event first-hand.
    11.2 - Falsely KOSing a player will result in players calling a KOS on you, even if they are the Detective.
    11.3 - Throwing grenades that have the ability to harm players, without proof of traitorous actions, is not permitted.
    11.4 - When disarming or setting off explosives, give sufficient warning to your location and when you will do so.
    11.5 - Do not prop kill (intentionally kill or injure with props).

  • 12. Invalid grounds to kill or shoot someone.

    12.1 - Suspicious activity.
    12.2 - The player is AFK or not moving.
    12.3 - Not responding to a life check, unless in overtime.
    12.4 - Being near an unidentified body or refusing to identify it.
    12.5 - Being hit in crossfire.
    12.6 - Shooting someone unannounced.
    12.7 - You think they activated a T-Trap.
    12.8 - They have entered a room that you "claimed".
    12.9 - They are following you, we do not have a 3 warnings systems on this server.
    12.10 - They share a skin, hat or accessory of someone you saw.
    12.11 - They have been high sussed, unless it is overtime when the high suspicion becomes a KOS (Kill on Sight).

  • 13. Do not destroy detective equipment or testers.

    13.1 - Destroying detective equipment or moving it to where it is inaccessible is a KOSable offence. Detective equipment includes but is not limited to:
      13.1.1 - Health Station.
      13.1.2 - Peace Sentry.
      13.1.3 - Portable Tester.
    13.2 - Destroying or rendering a Traitor tester inoperable is a KOSable offence, unless done pre-round.

  • 14. Announce having a traitor weapon.

    14.1 - Upon obtaining a T weapon, announce it in chat.
    14.2 - Failure to do so and carrying it will be considered traitor baiting.
